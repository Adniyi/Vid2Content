Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new features will be available immediately. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do unused conversions roll over to the next month?

No, unused conversions do not roll over to the next month. Your conversion quota resets at the beginning of each billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) as well as PayPal. For Enterprise plans, we can also accommodate bank transfers and purchase orders.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No, all our plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment required. Annual plans are billed once per year but can be canceled anytime with a prorated refund.

What happens if I exceed my monthly conversion limit?

If you reach your monthly conversion limit, you won't be able to convert additional videos until your quota resets. You can upgrade your plan at any time to increase your limit.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for nonprofits, educational institutions, and students. Please contact our sales team for more information.
